# Cure My Boredom by Squash
# Roster
Jason Chan (PM), Aoanul Hoque, Isabel Zheng, Maya Berchin
# Description
Cure My Boredom is a website specially designed to entertain you in less than five minutes, guaranteed. Users can create an account and explore our three ways to entertainment: jokes, trivia, and activities. Users can accumulate points based on how much they play and compete with each other on the global leaderboard on who has cured their boredom the most.
